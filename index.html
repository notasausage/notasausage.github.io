A bit more about Patrick.

Patrick lives in Rochester, NY with his wonderful wife Jenna and his annoying mischievous dog Milo. He’s been designing & developing for the web since the Great Browser Wars™ of the 90s, when Apple was building Newtons instead of iPhones. He enjoys attending and speaking at conferences, where you can often find him wearing his KERN sweatshirt.

Patrick’s Previous Experience.

Currently working with clients like BMW & MINI Financial Services, Mederma and Delta, Patrick designs and builds enjoyable experiences across email, the web and mobile applications as an Experience Designer at Partners + Napier. He’s previously worked at Litmus, Harvard University and RIT.
